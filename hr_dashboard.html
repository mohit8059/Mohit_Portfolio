HR Analytics Dashboard

Business Problem

HR department wanted to analyze employee attrition, department-wise performance and workforce trends.

Tools Used

  • Power BI
  • Excel
  • DAX

KPIs

  • Total Employees
  • Attrition Rate
  • Average Salary
  • Average Age

Dashboard Features

  • Attrition Analysis
  • Department Analysis
  • Age Group Analysis
  • Salary Analysis
  • Gender Distribution

Key Insights

  • Highest attrition found in Sales Department.
  • Young employees showed higher turnover.
  • Salary had direct impact on retention.
