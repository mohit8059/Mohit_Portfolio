Financial Analysis Dashboard

Business Problem

Management needed a dashboard to compare Budget vs Actual performance and monitor profitability.

Tools Used

  • Power BI
  • Excel

KPIs

  • Total Revenue
  • Total Expenses
  • Total Profit
  • Budget Variance

Dashboard Features

  • Revenue Trend
  • Expense Analysis
  • Profit Analysis
  • Budget vs Actual
  • Monthly Comparison

Key Insights

  • Revenue exceeded budget in Q3.
  • Expenses increased during year-end.
  • Profit margin improved by 12%.
