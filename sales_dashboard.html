Sales Performance Dashboard

Business Problem

Management wanted a centralized dashboard to monitor sales, profit, orders, products and regional performance.

Tools Used

  • Power BI
  • Excel
  • DAX

KPIs

  • Total Sales
  • Total Profit
  • Total Orders
  • Profit Margin

Dashboard Features

  • Sales Trend Analysis
  • Regional Performance
  • Category Analysis
  • Top Products Analysis
  • Interactive Filters

Key Insights

  • Technology category generated highest revenue.
  • West region showed maximum profit.
  • Sales increased significantly in Q4.
